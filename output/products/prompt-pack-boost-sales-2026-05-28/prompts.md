# Boost Sales Prompt Pack
## How to Use
This prompt pack is designed to help you work with LLMs (Claude, GPT, Gemini) in the Boost Sales domain. To use these prompts, simply copy and paste them into your LLM interface and provide any necessary context or input. The prompts are labeled by use case, such as research, drafting, analysis, and automation, to help you quickly find the right prompt for your task. Review the prompts and adjust them as needed to fit your specific needs.
