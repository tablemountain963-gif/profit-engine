### Playbook
1. **Research your prospect's company**: Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator or Crunchbase to find company size, revenue, and job openings, like "Acme Inc. has 500 employees and $100M revenue".
2. **Personalize your subject line**: Use the prospect's first name and company name, like "Hi [First Name], saw your post on [Company Blog]".
3. **Keep your email body under 125 words**: Use Hemingway Editor or Grammarly to simplify your language and shorten your paragraphs, like "We help [Company Size] companies like [Similar Company] increase sales by 15%".
4. **Use social proof in your email signature**: Add a logo or testimonial from a similar company, like "Trusted by [Similar Company] and [Industry Leader]".
5. **Follow up with a phone call**: Use a tool like PhoneBurner or RingCentral to schedule a call 3 days after the email, like "Hi [First Name], following up on my email about [Topic]".
6. **Use a clear call-to-action**: Use a tool like Calendly or ScheduleOnce to schedule a meeting, like "Schedule a 15-minute call to discuss [Topic]".
7. **Segment your email list**: Use a tool like Mailchimp or Klaviyo to segment your list based on job title or company size, like "Send email A to CEOs and email B to Marketing Managers".
8. **Track your email metrics**: Use a tool like Yesware or Streak to track open rates, response rates, and click-through rates, like "Email A had a 25% open rate and 5% response rate".
9. **A/B test your email subject lines**: Use a tool like VWO or Unbounce to test two subject lines, like "Subject Line A vs. Subject Line B".
10. **Use a CRM to manage your leads**: Use a tool like Hubspot or Pipedrive to track your leads and follow-ups, like "Lead [First Name] is in stage [Stage Name]".

### Templates
* **Introduction Email**: "Hi [First Name], I saw your post on [Company Blog] and wanted to reach out about [Topic]. We help [Company Size] companies like [Similar Company] increase sales by [Percentage]. Best, [Your Name]"
* **Follow-up Email**: "Hi [First Name], following up on my email about [Topic]. We've had success with [Similar Company] and I think we could help you too. Schedule a call to discuss: [Calendly Link]"
* **Meeting Confirmation Email**: "Hi [First Name], looking forward to our call on [Date] at [Time]. We'll discuss [Topic] and how we can help you achieve [Goal]. Best, [Your Name]"
* **Nurture Email**: "Hi [First Name], wanted to share an article about [Topic] that I think you'll find interesting. We're experts in [Industry] and can help you with [Challenge]. Best, [Your Name]"
* **Break-up Email**: "Hi [First Name], it's been a while since we last spoke. If you're not interested in [Topic], no worries. We'll remove you from our list. Best, [Your Name]"
