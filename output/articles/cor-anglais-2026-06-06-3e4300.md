The cor anglais, also known as the English horn, is a double-reed woodwind instrument that plays a crucial role in many classical music compositions. ## Overview
The cor anglais is a larger and longer version of the oboe, with a warmer and more mellow sound. It is typically used in orchestral and chamber music settings to add depth and complexity to the overall sound. The instrument's unique sound is produced by the vibration of a double reed, which is made from two thin pieces of cane tied together. The cor anglais is often used to play melodic lines, but it can also be used to add harmony and texture to the music.
