Hyundai Motor Group has acquired SoftBank's remaining 9.65% stake in Boston Dynamics for $325 million, completing its full ownership of the company. This acquisition marks a significant milestone in the robotics industry, with Boston Dynamics' humanoid robot, Atlas, set to be used at a vehicle plant by 2028. 

## Overview
Boston Dynamics is a leading robotics company known for its innovative and advanced robots, including the Atlas humanoid robot. The company has been at the forefront of robotics research and development, with its robots being used in various applications such as search and rescue, manufacturing, and logistics. With Hyundai's acquisition, Boston Dynamics is expected to expand its capabilities and explore new applications for its robots.

## Why It Matters
The acquisition of Boston Dynamics by Hyundai is significant because it highlights the growing importance of robotics in the automotive industry. The use of Atlas humanoid robots at vehicle plants by 2028 is expected to improve efficiency and reduce costs. Additionally, the acquisition demonstrates the increasing interest of major automotive companies in robotics and artificial intelligence. This trend is expected to continue, with more companies investing in robotics and AI research and development.
