## Common Pitfalls
One common pitfall to watch out for when using the Asvine C2000 fountain pen is hard starts. This is when the pen has difficulty starting to write, often due to the ink not flowing properly. To avoid this, make sure to clean the pen regularly and use a high-quality ink. Another pitfall is ink leakage, which can be prevented by properly closing the pen when not in use.
