One common pitfall marketing agencies face when using AI visibility tools is relying too heavily on automation. While AI can streamline many tasks, human intuition and creativity are still essential for developing effective marketing campaigns. Another pitfall is failing to integrate AI visibility tools with other marketing strategies, such as content creation and social media management. This can lead to a disjointed approach that fails to deliver consistent results.
