In fact, the industry's success can be attributed to the creative "hacking" of medical equipment and the use of algorithmic techniques to optimize results. This approach has allowed Turkish clinics to offer high-quality hair transplants at a significantly lower cost than their European and American counterparts, making them a magnet for medical tourists.

The transplant industry, particularly the hair transplant sector, has experienced rapid growth in recent years. Furthermore, the rise of medical tourism has created new challenges, such as ensuring that patients receive adequate care and support in a foreign country.
