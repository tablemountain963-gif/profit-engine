## Overview
The Tank Man incident occurred on June 5, 1989, during a time of great upheaval in China. The protests in Tienanmen Square, which began as a memorial for the deceased General Secretary of the Communist Party, Hu Yaobang, had evolved into a broader movement for democratic reforms. The Chinese government, however, saw these protests as a threat to their authority and decided to intervene militarily. It was in this context that Tank Man, whose real name remains unknown, stepped forward to confront the advancing tanks.
