## Overview
Earth Tones are a collection of colors inspired by the natural world, including shades of brown, beige, sienna, umber, and ochre. These colors are often used in art, design, and home decor to create a sense of warmth, coziness, and connection to the environment. The Earth Tone palette is characterized by its muted, earthy quality, which can add depth and character to any space.
