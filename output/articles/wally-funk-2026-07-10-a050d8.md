## Overview
Wally Funk was part of a group of 13 women who underwent astronaut training in the 1960s, known as the Mercury 13. Despite passing the rigorous physical and mental tests, they were denied the opportunity to fly to space due to their gender. It wasn't until 2021, at the age of 82, that Funk finally got her chance to travel to space aboard a Blue Origin New Shepard spacecraft. Her journey to space was a culmination of a lifetime of waiting, and her story has inspired countless individuals around the world.
