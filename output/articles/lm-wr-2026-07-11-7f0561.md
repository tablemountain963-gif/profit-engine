## Common Pitfalls
One common pitfall in photography is using the wrong camera settings for the situation. For example, using a high ISO in bright lighting conditions can result in noisy photos, while using a low ISO in low-light conditions can result in underexposed photos. Another common pitfall is not investing in good camera equipment, such as a **prime lens**, which can provide better image quality than a kit lens. Additionally, not taking the time to learn about photography techniques and best practices can also hinder your progress as a photographer.
