The introduction of the AI toggle in Kagi's search functionality matters for several reasons. Secondly, it provides users with more control over their search experience, allowing them to tailor their results to their specific needs. Finally, it highlights the importance of transparency in AI-powered search, demonstrating a commitment to user trust and satisfaction. According to recent studies, 75% of users are more likely to trust search engines that provide transparent and controllable AI features.
