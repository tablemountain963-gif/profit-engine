## Common Pitfalls
One of the most common pitfalls in financial management is the lack of a clear plan or strategy. Without a well-defined goal, individuals may struggle to make progress or achieve their financial objectives. By building an emergency fund and diversifying investments, individuals can mitigate these risks and ensure long-term financial stability.
