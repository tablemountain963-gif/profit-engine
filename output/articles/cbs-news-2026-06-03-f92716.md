The battle for CBS News is crucial because it reflects a broader trend in the media industry. As tech oligarchs acquire traditional news sources, they can shape the narrative and influence public opinion. According to some analysts, this approach can lead to a "zombie" version of the original institution, where the outward appearance remains the same, but the inner workings are fundamentally altered.
