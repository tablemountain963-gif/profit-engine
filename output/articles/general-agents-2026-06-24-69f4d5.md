## Overview
General agents refer to autonomous systems that can perform a wide range of tasks, from simple data processing to complex decision-making. These agents are designed to learn, adapt, and interact with their environment, making them a crucial component of various applications, including robotics, healthcare, and finance. Recent advancements in language world models have enabled general agents to better understand and respond to human language, paving the way for more sophisticated and human-like interactions.
