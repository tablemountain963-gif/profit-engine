## Overview
The HDMI Consortium is a group of companies that work together to develop and promote the HDMI standard. Founded in 2003, the Consortium has grown to include major players in the tech industry, all working towards a common goal: to create a universal standard for high-definition multimedia interfaces. By establishing a set of guidelines and testing procedures, the HDMI Consortium ensures that devices from different manufacturers can work seamlessly together, providing consumers with a hassle-free experience.
