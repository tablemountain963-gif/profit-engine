## How to Start
To get started with creating an AI music video, you'll need to select a suitable AI model and provide it with the necessary inputs, including the song, budget, and tools. The model will then take over, researching concepts, planning, and executing the music video. It's essential to define clear parameters and guidelines for the AI model to ensure the output meets your expectations. This may involve specifying the style, tone, and overall aesthetic of the music video.

## Common Pitfalls
While AI music video production offers numerous benefits, there are also potential pitfalls to watch out for. One common issue is the lack of creativity and originality in the output, as the AI model may rely on familiar patterns and tropes. Additionally, the quality of the output may vary depending on the model and inputs used. It's crucial to carefully evaluate the results and refine the process as needed to achieve the desired outcome.
