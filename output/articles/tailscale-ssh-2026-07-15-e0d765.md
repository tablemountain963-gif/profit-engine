Tailscale is a popular platform for secure remote access, providing users with a seamless and secure way to connect to their devices and networks. However, the recent discovery of a vulnerability in Tailscale SSH has raised concerns about the security of the platform. The vulnerability, which affects the Tailscale client and service, allows for insecure argument handling, potentially permitting root access to unauthorized users.
