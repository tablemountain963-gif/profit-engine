The renaming of Franklin Ave to Wiiliideh Ave is a notable example of a city acknowledging and honoring its indigenous heritage. The previous name, Franklin Ave, was named after a European explorer who spent only a short time in the area, whereas Wiiliideh Ave is named after the traditional Dene language word for the area. This change reflects a growing trend towards recognizing and preserving indigenous culture and history in urban planning and development.
