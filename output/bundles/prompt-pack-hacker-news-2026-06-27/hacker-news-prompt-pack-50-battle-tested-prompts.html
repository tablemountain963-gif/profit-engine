How to Use

To get the most out of these prompts, simply copy and paste them into your preferred LLM interface, replacing [BRACKETED] variables with your specific inputs. This will help you unlock the full potential of Hacker News and streamline your workflow with tools like Claude, GPT, and Gemini.

Research

  1. Analyze the top 10 comments on a Hacker News post with [NUMBER] comments to identify common themes and sentiment using GPT. Replace [NUMBER] with the actual comment count. Examine the post's title and content for context. Use Claude to summarize the findings.
  2. Use Gemini to extract all links from a Hacker News post about [TOPIC] and group them by domain. Identify the most frequently shared domains. Create a list of the top 5 domains and their corresponding link counts.
  3. Identify the most active users on Hacker News in the past [TIMEFRAME] months, focusing on those who have commented on posts related to [INDUSTRY]. Use GPT to analyze their comment history and engagement patterns.
  4. Compare the engagement metrics (e.g., comments, upvotes) of two Hacker News posts about [COMPETING_TECHNOLOGIES]. Determine which post performed better and why, using Claude for analysis.
  5. Develop a list of the top [NUMBER] Hacker News posts from the past [TIMEFRAME] weeks, filtered by [CATEGORY]. Use Gemini to summarize each post's key points and main discussion topics.

Drafting

  1. Write a Hacker News post title about [TOPIC] that attracts [NUMBER] upvotes, using GPT's predictive capabilities. Consider the post's content and target audience. Optimize the title for maximum engagement.
  2. Create a draft of a Hacker News comment responding to a post about [CONTROVERSIAL_TOPIC], taking a [STANCE] position. Use Claude to refine the tone and clarity of the comment. Ensure it aligns with Hacker News' community guidelines.
  3. Use Gemini to generate a Hacker News post about [NEW_TECHNOLOGY], including [NUMBER] examples of its applications. Research the technology's current state and future prospects. Write a compelling introduction to the post.
  4. Compose a Hacker News submission about [STARTUP_NAME], highlighting its [KEY_FEATURES] and [UNIQUE_VALUE_PROPOSITION]. Utilize GPT to craft a persuasive and informative post. Include relevant links and images.
  5. Develop a series of [NUMBER] Hacker News posts exploring different aspects of [INDUSTRY_TREND], using Claude to research and outline each post. Create a content calendar to schedule the posts.

Analysis

  1. Evaluate the sentiment of Hacker News comments on a post about [COMPANY_NAME], using GPT to identify [NUMBER] common themes and emotions. Determine the overall sentiment and its implications.
  2. Use Gemini to analyze the engagement metrics of a Hacker News post about [TOPIC], comparing its performance to similar posts. Identify factors contributing to its success or failure.
  3. Identify the most influential users on Hacker News who have commented on posts related to [INDUSTRY], using Claude to assess their impact. Develop a list of the top [NUMBER] influencers and their corresponding influence scores.
  4. Compare the content and tone of two Hacker News posts about [COMPETING_TECHNOLOGIES], determining which approach resonates better with the audience. Utilize GPT to analyze the posts' language and structure.
  5. Develop a framework for evaluating the credibility of sources cited in Hacker News posts about [TOPIC], using Gemini to assess [NUMBER] examples. Establish criteria for credible sources and apply them to the examples.

Automation

  1. Create a script using Gemini to automatically extract and summarize the top [NUMBER] comments from a Hacker News post about [TOPIC]. Set up a notification system to alert when new comments are posted.
  2. Use GPT to generate a daily digest of the top [NUMBER] Hacker News posts, filtered by [CATEGORY]. Include a brief summary of each post and its main discussion topics.
  3. Develop a bot that uses Claude to respond to basic questions about [TOPIC] on Hacker News, providing [NUMBER] pre-defined answers. Implement a mechanism to escalate complex questions to human moderators.
  4. Automate the process of submitting new Hacker News posts about [INDUSTRY_TREND], using Gemini to generate [NUMBER] post titles and summaries. Schedule the posts for publication at optimal times.
  5. Use Claude to monitor Hacker News for posts about [COMPANY_NAME], sending alerts to [EMAIL_ADDRESS] when new posts are detected. Include a summary of the post and its main discussion topics in the alert.
