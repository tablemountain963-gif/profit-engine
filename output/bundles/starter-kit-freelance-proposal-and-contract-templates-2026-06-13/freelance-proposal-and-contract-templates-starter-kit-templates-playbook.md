### Playbook
1. Define your niche using tools like Ahrefs and SEMrush to identify high-demand services, e.g., "I specialize in writing e-commerce product descriptions for Shopify stores".
2. Develop a unique value proposition (UVP) using the framework from Strategyzer, e.g., "I help e-commerce businesses increase sales by 25% through compelling product descriptions".
3. Create a portfolio using platforms like Behance and Contently, showcasing 3-5 case studies that demonstrate your UVP, e.g., "Increased sales by 30% for a fashion brand through product description rewriting".
4. Set clear boundaries and expectations with clients using tools like Calendly and Trello, e.g., "I'm available for meetings on Tuesdays and Thursdays, and respond to emails within 2 hours".
5. Use a contract management tool like PandaDoc to streamline proposal and contract signing, reducing the time spent on administrative tasks by 40%.
6. Develop a pricing strategy using the "value-based pricing" approach from Jonathan Stark, e.g., "I charge $100 per product description, based on the potential revenue increase for the client".
7. Establish a client onboarding process using tools like Zoom and Asana, e.g., "I schedule a 30-minute onboarding call to discuss project goals and timelines".
8. Monitor and adjust your proposal and contract process using data from tools like Google Analytics and Mixpanel, e.g., "I track the conversion rate of proposals and adjust my UVP and pricing strategy accordingly".
