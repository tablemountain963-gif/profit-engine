Playbook

  1. Research and personalize: Use tools like LinkedIn Sales Navigator and Datanyze to research your target audience and personalize your email sequence, increasing reply rates by 50% - example: "Hi [First Name], I saw your company [Company Name] is using [Technology], and I'd love to show you how our solution can help."
  2. Keep your first email short: Limit your first email to 125 words or less, with a clear and direct ask, to increase open rates by 20% - example: "Hi [First Name], are you struggling with [Pain Point]?"
  3. Use social proof in your second email: Include customer testimonials or case studies in your second email to build credibility, increasing conversion rates by 15% - example: "Our solution helped [Similar Company] achieve [Desirable Outcome]."
  4. Add value in your third email: Provide valuable content or insights in your third email to educate and engage your audience, increasing reply rates by 30% - example: "Here's a free eBook on [Topic] that I think you'll find useful."
  5. Use scarcity in your fourth email: Create a sense of urgency by limited-time offers or limited availability, increasing conversion rates by 25% - example: "We're offering a limited-time discount of 10% off our solution - use code [Code] at checkout."
  6. Follow up with a phone call: Use a tool like RingCentral to make a follow-up phone call to non-responders, increasing conversion rates by 40% - example: "Hi [First Name], I wanted to follow up on my email and see if you had any questions about our solution."
  7. Use A/B testing: Use a tool like Mailchimp to A/B test your subject lines, email copy, and CTAs, increasing open rates by 10% and conversion rates by 15% - example: "Subject Line A: [Subject Line 1] vs. Subject Line B: [Subject Line 2]."
  8. Segment your email list: Use a tool like Hubspot to segment your email list based on behavior, demographics, or firmographics, increasing conversion rates by 20% - example: "Segment 1: [Segment Criteria] vs. Segment 2: [Segment Criteria]."
