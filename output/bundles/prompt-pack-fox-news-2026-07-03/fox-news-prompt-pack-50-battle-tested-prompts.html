How to Use

To get the most out of these prompts, fill in the [BRACKETED] variables with your specific needs and use the outputs to inform your understanding of Fox News. Start by selecting a prompt that aligns with your goal, and then use the resulting output as a foundation for further research or content creation.

Research

  1. Use Claude to analyze the sentiment of Fox News articles published in [MONTH] about [TOPIC], and summarize the findings in a 2-paragraph report. Be sure to include the number of positive, negative, and neutral articles. Compare the results to [PREVIOUS_MONTH] to identify trends. The report should include at least 3 specific article titles.
  2. GPT can help you identify Fox News segments that feature [GUEST_NAME] as a commentator, by searching through transcripts from [START_DATE] to [END_DATE]. Provide a list of 10 segments, including the date, segment title, and a brief summary of the discussion. Use this information to analyze [GUEST_NAME]'s recurring themes and opinions.
  3. I want to understand the coverage of [EVENT_NAME] on Fox News, specifically the number of articles and segments dedicated to it between [START_DATE] and [END_DATE]. Use Gemini to gather this data and present it in a table, including the title, date, and author of each article, as well as the segment title and guest list for each segment. Compare the coverage to [COMPETING_NETWORK].
  4. Create a list of the top 10 Fox News opinion pieces from [YEAR] that discuss [POLICY_ISSUE], using Claude to rank them by the number of reader comments. Include the title, author, and publication date of each piece, as well as a brief summary of the main argument presented. Analyze the common themes and arguments presented in these opinion pieces.
  5. Use GPT to track the number of times [KEYWORD] is mentioned on Fox News between [START_DATE] and [END_DATE], and provide a graph showing the trend over time. Compare this to the mentions on [COMPETING_NETWORK] during the same period. Identify any notable spikes or patterns in the data.

Drafting

  1. Write a script for a Fox News segment about [TOPIC] using Gemini, including an introduction, 3 interview questions for [GUEST_NAME], and a conclusion. The script should be 2-3 minutes long and include a brief summary of the key points to be discussed. Use this script as a starting point for a more in-depth exploration of [TOPIC].
  2. I need a draft of a Fox News article about [EVENT_NAME], including a summary of the event, quotes from [KEY_FIGURE], and analysis from [ANALYST_NAME]. Use Claude to generate a 500-word draft, and then revise it to include at least 3 specific examples and a clear thesis statement. Ensure the article is well-structured and engaging.
  3. Use GPT to create an outline for a Fox News documentary about [HISTORICAL_EVENT], including 5 key sections and a list of potential interviewees. The outline should include a brief introduction, a detailed analysis of the event, and a conclusion that summarizes the key points. Identify potential visual elements and archival footage to include in the documentary.
  4. Draft a social media post for Fox News about [BREAKING_NEWS], using Gemini to suggest 3 different options with varying tones and hashtags. Each post should include a brief summary of the news, as well as a call to action for followers to share their thoughts and engage with the content. Analyze the potential engagement and reach of each post.
  5. Create a Fox News editorial about [POLICY_ISSUE] using Claude, including 3 key arguments and a rebuttal to [COUNTERARGUMENT]. The editorial should be 750 words long and include at least 5 specific examples and data points to support the arguments. Ensure the tone is clear and persuasive.

Analysis

  1. Analyze the tone of Fox News anchors when discussing [TOPIC] using GPT, and compare it to the tone used by [COMPETING_NETWORK] anchors. Present the findings in a table, including the anchor's name, date, and a brief summary of the discussion. Identify any notable patterns or differences in tone.
  2. Use Gemini to identify the most common [ADJECTIVE] words used by Fox News commentators when describing [POLITICIAN_NAME], and provide a word cloud to visualize the results. Analyze the potential implications of this language on public perception of [POLITICIAN_NAME].
  3. I want to understand the impact of Fox News coverage on [EVENT_NAME], specifically the number of viewers who reported being influenced by the coverage. Use Claude to gather data on the event's viewership and provide a report on the findings, including at least 3 specific statistics and a brief analysis of the results.
  4. Compare the coverage of [TOPIC] on Fox News and [COMPETING_NETWORK] using GPT, including the number of articles, segments, and tone of the coverage. Present the findings in a graph, including the date, title, and author of each article and segment. Identify any notable differences in coverage.
  5. Use Claude to analyze the language used by Fox News pundits when discussing [ISSUE], including the frequency of [KEYWORD] and the tone of the discussion. Provide a summary of the findings, including at least 2 specific examples and a brief analysis of the implications.
